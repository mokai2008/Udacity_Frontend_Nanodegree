Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. Their legs alone are taller than many humans.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Length: 420 centimetres
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Savanna
